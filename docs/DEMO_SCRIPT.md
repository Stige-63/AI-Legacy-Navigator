# Demo Script (for Sheron's video)

Target length: ~2–3 minutes.

## 1. Intro (15–20 sec)

> "Hi, I'm Sheron Burton, founder of the Youth Legacy Initiative. This is AI Legacy Navigator — a
> tool that helps young people discover their strengths, explore their possibilities, and build
> their legacy."

## 2. Welcome screen (10 sec)

- Show the Welcome screen.
- Point out the product name, program name, tagline, and the educational-use disclaimer.
- Click **Start**.

## 3. Questionnaire (30–40 sec)

- Fill in a sample profile on screen, narrating as you go:
  - Preferred name: e.g. "Jordan"
  - Grade range
  - Three interests (e.g. basketball, video editing, helping younger kids)
  - A strength, a challenge, a community concern, and a future goal
  - Preferred pathway
  - One admired person and why
- Note: "Notice we never ask for a last name, address, or school — just enough to reflect back
  something meaningful."
- Click **See My Legacy Profile**.

## 4. Legacy Profile (20–30 sec)

- Walk through the Strength Summary, Interest Themes, Values & Motivation Themes, and Legacy
  Direction.
- Read the encouraging statement aloud.
- Emphasize: "This is a reflection, not a label — it's built directly from what the user shared."

## 5. Pathway Recommendations (20–30 sec)

- Scroll through the three pathway cards.
- Highlight the "why it fits," first step, skill to develop, and trusted adult for one card.

## 6. 30-Day Action Plan (20–30 sec)

- Show the four weekly themes (Discover, Learn, Practice, Share or Serve).
- Point out the measurable goal, action steps, reflection question, and accountability person.

## 7. Results & Export (15–20 sec)

- Click **Copy Results** and **Print / Save as PDF**.
- Click **Start Over** to show the flow resets cleanly.

## 8. Closing (10–15 sec)

> "AI Legacy Navigator is a Build Week MVP from the Youth Legacy Initiative — simple, safe, and
> ready to help youth turn self-reflection into action. Thank you."
